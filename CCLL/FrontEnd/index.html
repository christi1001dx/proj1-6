How Do You Stay Hungry When You Settle?
Imagine yourself wanting to take AP Calculus BC. Imagine that you diligently put the course down after rushing to find the Assistant Principal of the Math Department, pleading with her to let you in, and then waiting for the Fall when you find out whether you are in. Imagine finding out that you don't have the course for whatever reason, whether that reflect more on you or on the administration. In that circumstance, when you have put in everything you have, and that is not sufficient. Imagine being forced to settle, to not be able to take the class. In that scenario, how do you, stay hungry, how do you want more? That is the question Stuyvesant students contend with regularly.
